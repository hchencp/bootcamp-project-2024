Vietnamese Coffee Recipe

Posted on Dec 19, 2024

  • 2 tablespoons ground Vietnamese coffee
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place 2 tablespoons of ground Vietnamese coffee in a phin filter.
  2. Screw on the filter press or lightly press it down.
  3. Place the phin filter over a glass or cup.
  4. Pour a small amount of hot water (about 1 tablespoon) to let the coffee bloom for 30 seconds.
  5. Add the remaining hot water and allow the coffee to drip through the filter.
  6. Once all the water has dripped through, stir in the sweetened condensed milk.
  7. Serve hot, or pour over ice for an iced Vietnamese coffee.
