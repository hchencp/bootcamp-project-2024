Green Thai Tea Recipe

Posted on Nov 15, 2024

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons green tea leaves
  • 2 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk or coconut milk
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a medium-sized pot.
  2. Add green tea leaves, crushed cardamom pods, and the cinnamon stick to the boiling water.
  3. Reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
  4. Strain the tea into a heat-safe container to remove the tea leaves and spices.
  5. Stir in the sugar while the tea is still hot, adjusting to your preferred sweetness.
  6. Let the tea cool slightly, then pour it into a glass.
  7. Add the evaporated milk or coconut milk and stir well.
  8. Serve warm, or pour over ice cubes for a refreshing cold drink.
